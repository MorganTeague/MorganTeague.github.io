Welcome to Morgan Tech

Empowering communities through smart, accessible innovation

What is Morgan Tech?

Morgan Tech is a youth-driven tech initiative designing accessible smart tools for families often overlooked by mainstream innovation.

Our Core Values

  • Equity
  • Innovation
  • Empowerment
  • Simplicity
  • Community

Our Vision

We dream of a world where smart home tech is:

  • Inclusive
  • User-friendly
  • Supportive of independence
  • Improving daily life
  • Designed with community input

What We Do

  • Home Assist — A digital assistant for reminders, schedules, and care routines
  • Voice Access — Accessibility features for mobility or vision challenges
  • Youth-Led Innovation — Solutions made by and for underserved communities

Why It Matters

Technology should work for everyone. We build for families with unique needs—single parents, elders, and schools—because innovation belongs to all.
